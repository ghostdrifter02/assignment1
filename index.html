    Project Idea

    Overview

    My project idea will be a smart health consulting app that is made for patients who have previous health medical records that need constant attention or treatment and other individuals who need a general check-up. This app is made to help a patient book appointment with local general practitioners that are available and near the patient’s area. Also, the app has a local GPS tracker that identifies nearby services e.g., ambulance or local clinics that are available to arrive at your destination aid you. This app is user friendly and simple to use which enables all ages to interact with. Its main purpose is to help patients who need medical attention or other services, the app also consists of many other features that help an everyday patient. This is not specifically made for existing patients but other people too.

    Motivation

    This project idea will be useful to everyday people who do not have the time to go physically to the clinic and book an appointment as well as talking to the receptionist over the phone to book an appointment. However, booking an appointment through the health consultation app will become much more efficient and simpler than going to the local clinic as it takes away the hassle to wait in line. Another feature that would be useful is the real-time video consultation between the doctor and the patient through the application. According to OptiSol grandviewresearch “the global mHealth apps market size was valued at USD 12.4 billion in 2018 and is projected to expand at a CAGR of 44.7% over the forecast period”.

    Description

    The smart health consultation app has many features that are built into the app. One of the main important features is booking appointments with doctors who are available and near your area. An individual just simply needs to create an account, fill in their medical records and book an appointment with a doctor. Also, the patient has many options to choose from, whether it could be a specialist doctor or a general practitioner and also be able to choose a female or male doctor.

    Another important feature is the app has a GPS tracker that notifies nearby ambulance or clinics for any case of emergency as it saves time to call triple zero and speak to an automated machine transferring you either to police or ambulance. The patients are also able to rate the severity of the situation to quickly notify the ambulance of the emergency. The app has a map of all the local clinics and nearby ambulance that show the opening hours and contact details.

    An additional feature of the app is that it allows patients to store sensitive data like medical records and personal information into the app, no information is given to any other organisation or company without the consent of the patient. Not only are you able to store your medical data, but you can scan your heart rate or track your steps. Tracking your daily movements and intake of food can be tracked through the app. The doctor can only view your medical records with granted permission of the patient, the doctors can create meal prep ideas and suggestion on exercise that help improve your overall wellbeing by catering to your own needs and wants.

    The app also has a feature to message or video chat the desired doctor to examine the patient’s health and communicate with them on the health issues with real-time consultation. This further helps the patient feel more comfortable to freely express their health concerns with the doctor.

    The doctor can prescribe and send prescriptions to the patient online via the app. Which allows the patient to buy the prescription over the counter just by simply showing the prescription to the pharmacist

    Tools and Technologies

    The software that would be used to create this health consultation app is Bizness Apps as this software is the most appropriate for this project. The hardware that must be included for this app is a GPS receiver, touch ID, microphone, camera and accelerator. These are some of the appropriate hardware that is needed to create a health consultation app.

    The completion of successfully creating the project app will help individuals all around the world to find better care and also its right under their fingertips which makes it more accessible. By having a fully functional app, it solves the problem for patients to save time and interact with the doctors more comfortably without the pressure of going to the clinic and having an awkward conversation with them.
